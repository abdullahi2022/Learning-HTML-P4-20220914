Laying out the DIVs

Here is another container, illustrated by DIV
A DIV is a tag that puts a border around text like displayed in the text above.

You can customize a DIV in terms of what type of border it is, ex. circle/square and you can also change the color.

To edit the DIV, go to the style tag and type in div then {} and you can change the border and color from inside that tag.

You can have multiple DIVs and to override the style thats above, put the style directly in the DIV tag.
